About these repositories

I’m John Kerl, a software developer in the NYC area. I’ve been hard at work in the software industry for 20+ years; 99% of what I do is intellectual property for the firms I’ve worked for. Most of my career in transaction processing, embedded systems, and data engineering simply isn’t visible here.
